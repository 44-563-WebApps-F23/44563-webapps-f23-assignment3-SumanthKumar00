Rambha Chicken

I like this item because i'm a Non Vegetarian. And this particular item has a blend of both Chicken and Egg.
It has the creamy textured chicken gravy as in its making they use cashews and cream mainly. And the egg is used inthe form of an Omelette.
The creamy chicken gravy is covered by an Omelette and presented with a fine garnish. This item suits well with Roti and Nan.
That creamy Chicken with a piece of Omelette combined with Nan or Roti makes a taste bomb when eaten together.
